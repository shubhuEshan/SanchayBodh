# SanchayBodh 💸🧠

SanchayBodh is an AI-powered financial management application designed to help users track expenses, split costs seamlessly, and gain intelligent insights into their spending habits.

## 🚀 Features

- 💰 **Expense Tracking**
  - Add, categorize, and monitor daily expenses
  - Get a clear overview of where your money goes

- 🤝 **Split Expenses**
  - Split bills with friends and groups (Splitwise-style)
  - Track who owes whom with ease

- 📊 **Financial Summary**
  - Visual breakdown of spending patterns
  - Monthly and category-wise insights

- 🧠 **AI-Powered Insights**
  - Smart analysis of spending behavior
  - Identify unnecessary expenses
  - Personalized suggestions to save money

- 📈 **Financial Reports**
  - Detailed reports for better financial planning
  - Track improvements over time

## 🎯 Goal

To empower users with clarity and intelligence in managing their finances by combining traditional expense tracking with AI-driven insights.
